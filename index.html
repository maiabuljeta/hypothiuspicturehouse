ABOUT HYPOTHIUS PICTUREHOUSE

Hypothius Picturehouse, founded by Maia Buljeta, is a production company focused on creating transformative change by blending entertainment with real-world impact. The company's mission revolves around using storytelling to inspire and empower socially conscious innovators. Through its projects, Hypothius Picturehouse seeks to push boundaries and highlight pressing global issues, delivering content that is both thought-provoking and meaningful.

The company's approach to film and media production emphasizes sustainability and social responsibility. Hypothius Picturehouse is committed to creating a lasting influence by building sustainable futures. This focus goes beyond entertainment, as it seeks to directly contribute to real-world solutions and advocate for environmental and social causes through its creative work.

Driven by a visionary leadership style, Hypothius Picturehouse is not just a typical film production company but a platform for transformative storytelling. It aims to shape culture and society by merging creative narratives with a purpose, establishing a new standard for media that goes beyond passive consumption to engage audiences in the change they wish to see in the world.
